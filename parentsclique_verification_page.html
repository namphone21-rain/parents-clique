How Verification Works

We use a careful, human review process so members can feel safe while keeping wait times reasonable.

1

Request received

You complete the membership request form and provide 2–3 community references.

2

References contacted

ParentsClique emails the people you listed to ask whether they know and recommend you.

3

You help speed up the process

You should also let your references know that they may receive a verification email from us.

4

Review and approval

Once enough positive responses are received, your request may be approved.

5

Welcome email

If approved, you will receive a welcome email with your sign-up link.

Please note: Listing references does not guarantee approval. Our goal is to grow slowly, carefully, and with trust.
Why this matters:
  • Helps protect families and children
  • Builds a trusted local parent network
  • Keeps the community warm, respectful, and safe

ParentsClique is starting small so trust can grow naturally, one connection at a time.
